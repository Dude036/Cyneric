Known Magic Circle Phrases

Currently the way of teleporting between towns is setup via a series of Magic Circles in a select few towns. These phrases are closely guarded, but a few have slipped out. Most people use the train to commute, but the wealthy and the powerful use these. Here are the commonly known destinations

Using a Magic Circle

Step onto the circle, One person on the circle will state the phrase out loud, and all subjects on the circle will be instantly teleported to the Magic Circle destination you've chosen. Unless otherwise noted, the phrases are to be spoken in Common.
