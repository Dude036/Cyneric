Cast List

Every campaign I have been apart of always has one commonality. The Cast List. Since this is a world of fantasy, I ask my players to cast their characters as if it were a movie. Obviously, sometimes we have to take some creative liberties with characters of the non-humanoid variety, but it's all meant in good fun. This idea came from Troy Lavalee, from the Glass Cannon Podcast. Below is the cast list for all the characters in each of the major campaign's I have run.

  • Tyrant's Grasp Campaign
  • Era of Calamity Campaign
  • A Magical Investiture Campaign
  • Dragon Campaign
  • BXD Campaign
  • Cyneric Campaign
  • Thunia Campaign
  • Nethys' Revenge Campaign
